Privacy Policy for ScanPasteFill

Effective Date: January 2025

ScanPasteFill is a Microsoft Edge browser extension developed for internal healthcare and shipping workflows. It operates entirely within the user’s browser and does not transmit, collect, or share personal information outside the device.

Information Collection and Use

  • Profiles, preferences, and form data are stored locally using the chrome.storage.local API.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.
  • The extension does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking functionality.
  • Network access occurs only for standard Edge extension updates provided through Microsoft or your organization’s policy server.

Data Security

All operations occur locally in the browser environment. Users can remove all stored data at any time by uninstalling the extension.

Children’s Privacy

ScanPasteFill is intended for professional and internal enterprise use, not for children or general consumers.
